INSTRUCTIONS FOR LOCAL FONTS

In the final automated polishing step, the external Google Fonts links were removed to improve performance and remove strict CSP violations.

You MUST manually download the following fonts as `.woff2` files (e.g. from Google Webfonts Helper) and place them in this folder exactly identically named:

- dm-sans.woff2
- dm-mono.woff2
- instrument-serif-italic.woff2

This will ensure your styles and fonts instantly load and perform perfectly across local and production builds.
